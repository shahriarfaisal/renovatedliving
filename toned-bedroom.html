Patio Roof/Cover

This project was a complete demo of an existing spaced slat (flat) pergola cover and rebuild. A partial list of the items on this project includes: Construction of a rough cut gabeled cedar (hip) roof tying into the existing home, tounge and groove trim, decorative Hardware, electric including decorative indirect lighting, custom made window bar, staining, outdoor TV/stereo hookups, bench swing, decor, and concrete resurfacing.
